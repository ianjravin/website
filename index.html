About - Us

We are a customer engagement & experience business, developing channels that businesses use to engage their customers & enabling payments across all these channels. Channels include SMS, USSD, app, Web & social media We operate a scalable, omni-channel payments platform that processes payments,

manages devices & connects to various 3rd party systems & applications. We are an IBM business partner & provide services such as cognitive data analytics, predictive analytics, discovery through Watson(including chatbots) & API Management.
